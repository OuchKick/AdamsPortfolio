Hello!

My name is Adam and I currently live in Bryan, TX. I'm a recent graduate from the University of Texas Full Stack Bootcamp. When I'm not job hunting, learning about three.js or messing around with 3D modeling, you can find me rocking out to Afro Funk, taking pictures of trains or going down a YouTube rabbit hole. I'm also a keyboard fanatic.

My Story

My introduction to design came from a rather interesting gaming era; Modern Warfare 2 trickshotting. While looking at graphic designers for groups, I was amazed that people my age were learning 3D modeling and using Photoshop and I wanted to do it as well. So I did. I familiarized myself with Cinema 4D, Photoshop and After Effects.

Fast forward to now, I had been involved in the stock industry for a couple years. February 2021, the great Texas blizzard has struck and I had time to reflect. I was listening to an AM radio station while the electricity was out and heard, "do what you want to do and just try, don't live in fear." I took that as a sign. From then on, I've taken the same drive that I had as a kid to learn graphic design and pushed that into learning how to be an efficent web developer.

I'm a self-starter and I love to learn new things. If anyone would like to work on fun projects, please reach out!
